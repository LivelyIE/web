You will require your Employment Detail Summary for the most recent tax year which you can find on ros.ie.

If commission and bonus pay will be factored into your mortgage application, you will require your three most recent Employment Detail Summaries. Note that prior to 2019, this document was known as your P60.

Self Employed

If you are self employed, you will need in addition:

  • Your three most recent Form 11s.
  • Confirmation of your tax position from your accountant or a Charges & Payments Statement from Revenue Online.
