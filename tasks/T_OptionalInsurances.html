Other than mortgage protection insurance and home insurance, you will come across insurance products that are not legally required but may be useful to you.

While insurers will provide you with honest advice on what your options are, it's important to note that generally they will receive commission on insurance products you purchase.

Critical Illness Cover

Critical illness cover, or "serious illness" cover, is an optional addition to your mortgage protection policy whereby you will be payed a lump sum in the case that you develop a serious illness. You should read your policy carefully, and understand exactly which illnesses are covered as not all insurers are the same.
