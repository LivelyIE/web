It's easy to get wrapped up in the excitement of bidding on amazing house without considering if it's really the right one for you.

Before you begin you begin your search, take note of what you need and what you want. After all, you will likely be committing to your new home for many years.

Here are some factors you may want to consider:

  • Commute time.
  • Proximity to amenities.
  • Garden size and location.
  • Proximity of public transport.
  • Condition of the home.
  • Parking spots.
  • Number of bedrooms.
  • Local school options.
